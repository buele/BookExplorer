Welcome to Book Explorer

Introduction

Book Explorer is an open source iOS App to discover books and authors, using google freebase api.

Freebase is a large collaborative knoledge base consisting of metadata composed mainly by its community members (Wikipedia).

The Freebase APIs allow to tap into Google's Knoledge Graph
